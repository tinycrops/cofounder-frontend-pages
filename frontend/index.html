FAQ

Common questions

What is Cofounder?

Cofounder is an autonomous AI platform. You submit a business idea, and coordinated AI agents handle everything from market research to growth strategy to daily operations — running 24/7 without you needing to manage them.

How does it actually work?

When you submit an idea, four specialized agents activate: a CEO agent (strategy and priorities), a Research agent (competitive analysis and market sizing), a Growth agent (tasks, outreach, partnerships), and a Marketing agent (content, emails, social). They run in daily cycles, each building on the last.

What happens after I submit my idea?

Within minutes your company dashboard is live. You'll see your generated mission statement, competitive analysis, initial growth tasks, and draft outreach — all created autonomously. Agents continue running daily cycles from there.

Can I guide the agents?

Yes. Pro plan users can set custom agent instructions. All users can view agent output and adjust priorities through the dashboard. The agents are autonomous but not unsupervised.

What kind of ideas work best?

Anything with a clear customer and problem. SaaS products, local services, e-commerce, content businesses, marketplaces — the agents adapt to the domain. The more context you give, the sharper the output.
